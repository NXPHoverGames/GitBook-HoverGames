# Analog-digital converters

## ADC 3.3V connector pinout&#x20;

| Pin | Signal   | Voltage |
| --- | -------- | ------- |
| 1   | VCC      | +5.0V   |
| 2   | ADC IN 1 | +3.3V   |
| 3   | GND      | GND     |
| 4   | ADC IN 2 | +3.3V   |
| 5   | GND      | GND     |

## ADC 6.6V connector pinout

| Pin | Signal   | Voltage |
| --- | -------- | ------- |
| 1   | VCC      | +5.0V   |
| 2   | ADC IN 3 | +6.6V   |
| 3   | GND      | GND     |
